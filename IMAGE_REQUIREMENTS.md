# Image Requirements for Burwood Mechanics & Transmission Website

## Required Images

All images must be placed in `/assets/images/` directory with the filenames specified below.

### 1. **hero-transmission.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Hero section (main banner)
- **Purpose**: Large hero image showcasing transmission work or workshop scene
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 (1200x900px or larger, responsive)
- **Alt Text**: "Transmission rebuild specialist working on automatic transmission components in workshop"
- **Description**: Professional photo of transmission rebuild work in progress, or skilled mechanic working on transmission components. Should convey expertise and quality workmanship.

### 2. **mechanic-working.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Why Choose Us section (below the cards)
- **Purpose**: Showcase skilled mechanic performing detailed work
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (1600x900px or larger)
- **Alt Text**: "Skilled mechanic performing transmission work with precision tools and expertise"
- **Description**: High-quality image of mechanic using precision tools, working on transmission or mechanical components. Should highlight professionalism and attention to detail.

### 3. **classic-cars.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Vehicles section - Classic & Vintage card
- **Purpose**: Display classic and vintage vehicles
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (800x450px or larger)
- **Alt Text**: "Vintage and classic cars undergoing restoration and specialist maintenance"
- **Description**: Photo of classic/vintage vehicles (Holden, Triumph, Ford Mustang style) being serviced or restored. Shows care and expertise with heritage vehicles.

### 4. **passenger-cars.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Vehicles section - Modern Vehicles card
- **Purpose**: Display modern family vehicles
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (800x450px or larger)
- **Alt Text**: "Modern sedan and family passenger vehicles serviced by our mechanics"
- **Description**: Professional photo of modern sedans, hatchbacks, or family cars. Shows everyday vehicle maintenance.

### 5. **performance-cars.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Vehicles section - Performance & Sports card
- **Purpose**: Display performance/sports vehicles
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (800x450px or larger)
- **Alt Text**: "High-performance sports cars with enhanced engines and precision tuning"
- **Description**: Photo of sports cars or high-performance vehicles (e.g., Subaru, Holden V8, Porsche-style). Shows passion for performance vehicles.

### 6. **4x4-offroad.jpg** (Required)
- **Location**: Vehicles section - 4WD & Off-Road card
- **Purpose**: Display 4WD and adventure vehicles
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (800x450px or larger)
- **Alt Text**: "Off-road 4x4 vehicles and adventure trucks ready for challenging terrain"
- **Description**: Photo of 4WD vehicle, Land Rover, Toyota 4Runner, or adventure truck. Should convey ruggedness and capability.

## Image Guidelines

1. **Quality**: All images must be high-resolution (at least 1200px wide)
2. **Format**: JPEG or WebP format recommended for web
3. **Optimization**: Compress images for web (aim for < 500KB per image)
4. **Filenames**: Use lowercase with hyphens (e.g., `hero-transmission.jpg`)
5. **Authenticity**: Professional, realistic photos appropriate for Australian automotive workshop
6. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent color grading and lighting
7. **No Branding**: Avoid logos or watermarks of competitors

## Alternative Image Sources

- **Stock Photos**: Unsplash, Pexels, Pixabay (for transmission, mechanics, vehicles)
- **Professional Photography**: Hire local photographer to shoot at workshop
- **Licensed Stock**: Shutterstock, Getty Images (for professional workshop imagery)

## Responsive Images

The website uses CSS `object-fit: cover` to maintain aspect ratios on all devices. All images will:
- Display at full width on mobile
- Maintain aspect ratios automatically
- Load with lazy loading for performance
- Include proper alt text for accessibility

## Image Deployment

1. Add images to `/assets/images/` directory
2. Ensure filenames match exactly (lowercase, hyphens)
3. Test on mobile and desktop
4. Verify alt text appears in browser inspector
5. Check image loading performance using browser DevTools

## Notes

- Images can be updated anytime without modifying HTML or CSS
- URLs are relative paths (`/assets/images/filename.jpg`)
- All images have descriptive alt text for SEO and accessibility
- Featured service cards do not use images (only icons/text)
- Website is fully functional with placeholder images
