About Burwood Mechanics & Transmission

Burwood Mechanics & Transmission is a trusted independent workshop in Burwood, Inner West Sydney. We've built our reputation on honest advice, quality workmanship, and genuine care for every vehicle that comes through our doors.

Dave has decades of hands-on experience and a passion for getting things right. Whether you're driving a practical family sedan, restoring a classic, pushing the limits in a sports car, or tackling the outback in a 4x4, we have the expertise and integrity to keep your vehicle running at its best.

We believe in treating every customer with respect, explaining what needs to be done in plain language, and delivering results you can trust. That's why Burwood and Inner West residents have made us their preferred local mechanic for transmission work and general repairs.
